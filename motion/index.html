Q42 motion design tips

What makes good motion design? Purposeful clearly communicated movements; Consistently on-brand attitude; Intuitive, delightful and emotion evoking animation. All these things yes. There are many topics online which cover these specifics in great lenght. This design tips page will not be one of them.

This set of articles will showcase motion design tips and tricks in a practical and easy to digest way. A.k.a. the no bullshit approach.

Note: some of the terminology used in these articles are completely made up. In fact, everything is completely made up.
